About AI Recrutia

Recruitment made smarter — with people at the center.

Founded in 2015, AI Recrutia combines cutting-edge technology with human expertise to revolutionize the staffing industry. We believe in the power of personal connections and the potential of every individual.

Our mission is simple: to connect talented individuals with companies that value their skills and help them grow. We serve as the bridge between opportunity and potential, using data-driven insights to make perfect matches.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How does AI Recrutia differ from other staffing agencies?

AI Recrutia combines advanced AI matching technology with personalized human service. While our platform uses data to identify ideal matches quickly, our experienced recruiters ensure those matches align with cultural fit and career aspirations. This hybrid approach delivers both efficiency and the human touch that's often missing in digital recruitment.

Is there a cost for job seekers to use your services?

No, our services are completely free for job seekers. We're paid by companies when we successfully place candidates in roles. We'll never ask you for payment to access job opportunities or career support services.

How long does the recruitment process typically take?

The timeline varies depending on the role and your availability. On average, candidates receive their first interview requests within 3-7 business days of completing their profile. For urgent hiring needs, we've placed candidates in as little as 48 hours.

What industries do you specialize in?

We serve over 24 industries including healthcare, IT, construction, retail, manufacturing, and logistics. Our team includes specialists for each sector who understand the unique requirements and trends in their fields. Even if your industry isn't listed, we encourage you to reach out - we're constantly expanding our industry coverage.
