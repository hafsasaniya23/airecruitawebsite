Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recruitment process

Unlike many agencies that rely solely on algorithms, we combine technology with human expertise. Our recruiters take time to understand both candidates and employers personally, ensuring matches based on skills, culture fit, and long-term potential. We also provide ongoing support throughout the hiring process and beyond.

We specialize in healthcare, IT & technology, construction, retail, manufacturing, and logistics. Within these industries, we place professionals at all levels from entry-level to executive positions. Our recruiters have deep expertise in these specific sectors, allowing us to better understand the unique needs of both employers and job seekers.

No, our services are completely free for job seekers. Employers pay our fees when we successfully place a candidate with them. We never charge candidates for job placement, resume reviews, career counseling, or any other services we provide.

The timeline varies depending on the position and industry. For temporary or contract roles, we can often place candidates within 1-3 business days. For permanent positions, the process typically takes 1-3 weeks from initial contact to offer acceptance. We work efficiently while ensuring quality matches that benefit both parties long-term.

While we're based in Jersey City and serve the greater New York/New Jersey metropolitan area extensively, we also place candidates nationwide for many of our corporate clients. Some industries like healthcare and IT have particularly strong demand across multiple states, while others like construction tend to be more localized.
